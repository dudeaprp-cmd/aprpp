Methodology

How the Calculator Works

Every state begins with a Republican, Democrat, and Other baseline from CALC_STATE_BASELINES. National modifiers, lobbies, candidate experience, primary strength, debate wins, campaign points, approval environment, and ideology multipliers are then applied. Results are normalized back to 100 percent and EVs are awarded by state winner.
